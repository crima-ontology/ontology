# Main Modules

The **main modules** of the CRIMA ontology supports the representation of core climate risk-related concepts (e.g., hazards, impacts, exposures) in data and conceptual models, with grounding on top-level ontology notions (e.g., events, processes, qualities).

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

- [**ctlo module**](../modules/ctlo/ctlo.md)<br/>
  Top-level ontology notions not committing to a specific upper ontology

- [**ccore module**](../modules/ccore/ccore.md)<br/>
  Literature-grounded theory of climate risk to represent and link risk-related entities in data and conceptual models

- [**ich module**](../modules/ich/ich.md)<br/>
  Ontological representation of the conceptual risk model of *Impact Chains* [🔗](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-030-86211-4_25)

</div>
