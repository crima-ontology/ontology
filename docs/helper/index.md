# Helper Modules

The CRIMA ontology includes **helper modules** to represent information often co-occurring with risk-related conceptual models and data.
Currently, they provide bibliographic, geospatial, and identification concepts mostly relevant in the context of the [CRIMA project](https://crima.eurac.edu/).

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

- [**cbib module**](../modules/cbib/cbib.md)<br/>
  Bibliography-related concepts aligned to BIBO [🔗](https://dcmi.github.io/bibo/) and DCTERMS [🔗](https://www.dublincore.org/specifications/dublin-core/dcmi-terms/)

- [**cplace module**](../modules/cplace/cplace.md)<br/>
  Geospatial entity classes aligned to GeoSPARQL [🔗](https://www.ogc.org/standards/geosparql/), SOSA [🔗](https://www.w3.org/TR/vocab-ssn/) and DBpedia ontology [🔗](https://www.dbpedia.org/resources/ontology/)

- [**crid module**`](../modules/crid/crid.md)<br/>
  Concepts and properties for established identification systems (e.g., ZIP codes)

</div>
