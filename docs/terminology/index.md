# Terminology Modules

The **terminology modules** of the CRIMA ontology provide domain-specific classifications of risk-related concepts, such as hazards, impacts, adaptations, etc.
The use [SKOS](https://www.w3.org/TR/skos-reference/), and in particular `skos:Concept`, `skos:ConceptScheme`, and semantic relationships `skos:broader` and `skos:narrower`.
